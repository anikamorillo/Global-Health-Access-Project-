<section>
<h2>Mission Statement</h2>
<p>GHAP studies disparities in healthcare access worldwide through interviews and analysis of health systems. Findings highlight challenges, effective strategies, and resources for advocacy and education.</p>
</section>

<section>
<h2>Methodology</h2>
<ul>
<li>Interviews with doctors, nurses, and public health workers</li>
<li>Analysis of health system resources, policies, and outcomes</li>
<li>Ethical research with informed consent and anonymized participants</li>
</ul>
</section>

<section>
<h2>Impact & Goals</h2>
<p>GHAP raises awareness of healthcare inequities, promotes education and advocacy, and shares findings publicly to inspire solutions and highlight frontline healthcare experiences.</p>
